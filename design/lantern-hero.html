John 1
1In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God.
Observation
Type what you notice…
2He was with God in the beginning.
3Through him all things were made; without him nothing was made that has been made.
Personal
Type what you notice…
4In him was life, and that life was the light of all mankind.
Application
Light to walk by, not only to look at.
5The light shines in the darkness, and the darkness has not overcome it.
Personal
The dark never won. Hold onto that.
6There was a man sent from God whose name was John.
7He came as a witness to testify concerning that light.
Historical
The witness motif. John's Gospel is framed as testimony.
8He himself was not the light; he came only as a witness to the light.
Observation
"Light" three times in two verses. The theme is set.
9The true light that gives light to everyone was coming into the world.
10He was in the world, and though the world was made through him, the world did not recognize him.
Application
Made by him, and still missed. Slow down and look.
11He came to that which was his own, but his own did not receive him.
12Yet to all who did receive him, he gave the right to become children of God.
Personal
Receiving, not earning. That is the whole door.
13children born not of natural descent, nor of human decision, but born of God.
14The Word became flesh and made his dwelling among us.
Historical
"Dwelling" = tabernacled. A deliberate Exodus echo.
Quick note
Your study, kept beside the Word.
Hero v6: make two notes, then glide through a chapter full of your study. Loops.
